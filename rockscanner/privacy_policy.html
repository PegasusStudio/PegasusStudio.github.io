Pegasus Studio built the Rock Scanner app as a mobile app. This Service is provided by Pegasus Studio and is intended for use as is.

This page explains our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use Rock Scanner.

Information Collection and Use

Rock Scanner is designed to identify rocks and gemstones from photos using on-device scanning and offline reference information. The app does not require you to create an account to use its core scanning and field guide features.

The app may ask for access to your camera, photos, or device files so you can take or choose an image for scanning. Photos selected or captured for scanning are processed on your device for object detection and prediction. We do not upload your photos to our servers for identification.

If you keep prediction history enabled by using the app normally, Rock Scanner stores recent scan results and the cropped image used for prediction inside the app's local storage on your device. This history is used only so you can review previous predictions. You can delete saved prediction history from within the app.

The app includes an offline rock and gemstone information database bundled with the app. Looking up item details from this database does not require an internet connection and does not send a request to our servers.
